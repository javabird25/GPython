draw - screen drawing¶

gmod.draw.rounded_box(x, y, w, h, color: Union[gmod.color.Color, Tuple[int, int, int, int]], corner_radius)¶

Draws a rounded filled box.

Parameters:
  • x – the X coordinate of the upper-left corner of the box.
  • y – the Y coordinate of the upper-left corner of the box.
  • w – the box width, in other words, the X coordinate of the lower-right corner of the box.
  • h – the box height, in other words, the Y coordinate of the lower-right corner of the box.
  • color – the box fill color.
  • corner_radius – the radius of the rounded corners, works best with a multiple of 2.

Lua similar: draw.RoundedBox()
