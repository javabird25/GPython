loader - addon loader¶

loader.py is the second part of the initialization system.

Here is what it does:

  1. Redirects I/O to Garry’s Mod console with gmod.streams I/O classes.
  2. Patches the hook.Call Lua function to delegate hook calls to gmod.hooks module.
  3. Scans addons\ directory for PyGmod addons and initializes them.
loader.main()¶

Finishes the PyGmod initialization.
